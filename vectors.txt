Darkwave expands on the dark, introspective and sorrowful atmosphere of Gothic Rock; borrowing aspects from Electronic and other non-Rock influences.
Ethereal Wave refers to atmospheric guitar and synthesizer soundscapes, effects like reverb and delay, and commonly soprano female vocals to create a surreal, otherworldly effect.
Neoclassical Darkwave refers to Combines dark and melancholic atmosphere with Western Classical Music.
Neue Deutsche Todeskunst refers to Dark themes inspired by German philosophy and poetry, typically combining Electronic instrumentation with neoclassical influences and an eccentric, theatrical style.
